The second set of chords below is an attempt to capture the voicings Lewis used in
 an acoustic
Instagram performance, a brief clip of which can be found here:
https://www.youtube.com/shorts/N8JGnEsu1hM
 
He seems to play this in various ways (as he does with many of his songs).
 
For the video linked above, he had the capo on 6 and he did not jump the octave to
 sing the chorus.
To play these chords along with the official video, the capo should be on 4.
 
The notes in parentheses are just walking notes between chords and chords with an 
* indicate a single strum.
